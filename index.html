XBeeNG

Arduino library for communicating with XBees in API=2 mode, with sopport for both XBee Proprietary RF Modules and XBee ZigBee/802.15.4 RF Modules, with the classic 20 pin footprint.

XBee Proprietary RF Modules:

  • XBee-PRO® 900HP / XBee-PRO XSC (aka XB900HP).
  • XBee-PRO 868 (aka XB868).
  • XBee® DigiMesh® 2.4 (aka XBDM).

XBee ZigBee/802.15.4 RF Modules

  • XBee® ZB (aka XBZB).
  • XBee® 802.15.4 (aka XB802).
